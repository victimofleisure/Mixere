Playing Audio

Audio is played using the transport controls, which appear in the third column of a Mixere document, under the heading "Transport". The controls consist of four buttons:

PlayPlays the audio from its current position.
PauseStops the audio without rewinding it.
StopStops the audio and rewinds it.
LoopTurns loop mode on/off. Normally, a track plays until it reaches the end of the audio file, and then stops by itself. However, in loop mode, the audio plays continuously.

Various shortcuts are available:

  • To toggle back and forth between play and pause, press Space
  • To play, press P
  • To pause, press U
  • To stop, press S
  • To turn loop mode on/off, press L
